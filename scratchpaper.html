    What is Online Arbitrage?

    Online arbitrage is purchasing products from online retailers at lower prices and reselling them on Amazon for a profit.

    Getting Started: Step-by-Step

    1. Research Products
      • Use tools like Keepa or SellerAmp
      • Analyze sales rank and Buy Box
    2. Purchase Inventory
      • Buy from online retailers like Walmart, Kohls, Target, etc.
      • Ensure SKU's match
    3. List on Amazon
      • List inventory available on Amazon
      • Set competitive price
    4. Manage Fulfillment
      • Use FBA and send inventory to Amazon
      • Do FBM and handle own inventory
